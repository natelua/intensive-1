Creekside Farms

About Us

We are a small family-owned business located in the outer area of Watsonville, CA. We grow raspberries and blackberries on soil and on substrate ranches. Our harvest season runs from May through November of each year. We employ over 400 employees during our harvest season and downsize our operation in the winter months to about a 100 total employee count.

Work Requirements

To work at Creekside Farms, Inc., you simply need to love working outdoors. As an equal opportunity employer, we offer competitive wages and a pleasant work environment. Here at creekside you dont need any prior experience in berries as our team leaders are more than capable to teach. What we do look for while hiring is for our employees to be eager to learn!
